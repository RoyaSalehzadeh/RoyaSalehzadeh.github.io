Teaching

My teaching focuses on robotics, intelligent systems, and control, with an emphasis on hands-on learning, project-based assignments, and connecting theory to real robotic systems.

Courses

Unified Robotics III

Robot kinematics, sensing, and robotic system integration.

Unified Robotics IV

Advanced robotics topics including autonomy, perception, and intelligent decision making.

Mechatronic

Modeling of dynamic systems, feedback control, stability analysis, and controller design.

Statics

Fundamental principles of forces, equilibrium, and structural analysis used in engineering system design.

Artificial Intelligence

Core AI concepts with applications to robotics and intelligent systems.

Brain-Computer Interface

Explores neural signal acquisition and processing to enable direct communication and control between the human brain and external systems.

Teaching Approach

I aim to create an inclusive, supportive classroom where students build both conceptual understanding and practical skills. I use applied examples, structured milestones, and team projects to help students develop confidence in designing, implementing, and evaluating robotic systems.
