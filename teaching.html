Teaching

My teaching focuses on robotics, intelligent systems, and control with an emphasis on hands-on experimentation and system design.

  • Unified Robotics IV – advanced robotics, autonomy, and perception.
  • Control Systems – modeling of dynamic systems and feedback control.
  • Artificial Intelligence for Robotics – machine learning and decision-making for robotic systems.
